Optimisation Problem

$$ \min_{u_0, u_1, \cdots, u_{N-1}} J(x_0,...,x_{N+1},u_0, ... ,u_N) \nonumber \\ \mathrm{subject\;to} \; \quad x_0 = x_i, \ x_N \in \mathbb{X}, \nonumber \\ x_{k+1} = A_k x_k + B_k u_k, \\ x_k \in \mathbb{X}, \ u_k \in \mathbb{U}, \nonumber \\ \text{for all } k \in \{0,\, ...\, ,N-1\} \nonumber $$
